Who We Are?

Tyson Real Estate is, a prop-tech Company located in Dharan, Nepal serving society in an innovative way since Mangsir 15, 2077. It is a real estate agency devoted to consistently providing high customer service of buying, selling, and renting homes and properties, combined with a relaxing office atmosphere and excellent customer service. Tyson RealEstate as a marketing platform will serve everyone to simplify the real RealEstate scenario - removing the concept of the commission our valuable customers pay to the brokers. We are here trying to make everyone's life easier.

Our Service

Huge investments lead to a hassle and a chaotic situation in everyone’s life. We are here for your hassle-free investment and ‘Find – Buy/Sell/Rent you a property’. We will provide our clients with a network of buyers and sellers through a multiple listing service. We will be responsible for all the process as per your requirements.
